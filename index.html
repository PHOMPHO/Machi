Personality
Inspo
As someone who loves alternative and japanese street fashion with a touch of creepy cute, Machi is the culmination of that love! His biggest ties with real life fashion are mostly sweet lolita fashion and menhera.
Lolita is quite a well known style, but to be short and sweet, it's a subculture from Japan that is highly influenced by Victorian clothing and styles from the Rococo period with sweet lolita being a style of Lolita that contains features nearly exclusive to Lolita, it's also the most child-like of the styles, using many bows, ruffles and light colors to make up an outfit.
While menhera is a japanese slang term derived from "mental healther". It became the spark of a sickly cute aesthetic that takes the abiguous usage of the word "sick", like as an insult for something nonconformist, literally as reflection of the social stigma which still hides behind the surface of kawaii culture.
But, he also has some ties with an obscure art style, candy guro (which I don't recommend searching on poogle for...), which is an art style that tends to be more colourful, goopier, and sometimes "cuter" than normal guro art, it usually involves pastel and neon colour palettes.
His name is very much derived from that concept, with Candy obviously meaning candy and Machi meaning guro in japanese (and Byoki, his surname meaning sick also in japanese).

わたしにっついて

My name is Abyss (you can see my portfolio clicking here) and my pronouns are they/them, I'm a non-binary mortuary sciences student living in Brazil. I like writing, playing video games, cooking, collecting avatars, creating characters, photography and fashion. My favorite genre of media is horror, I love watching horror movies and playing horror themed games, and I would say my favorites in order are Army of Darkness and Silent Hill 2. In real life I have three cutesy little cats, Samara (10yo), Dothi (2yo) and Valeska (8mo) and a significant other that has been quite a light of hope in my life.
I've been on neopets since around 2009 and my journey with writing and creating characters began right here on this website! My first few characters were very inspired by (almost carbon copies of) Twilight, which, at the time, was very much my favorite book, but as I aged and was lead to explore other types of media I began to like other things, my writing style and my characters evolved alongside me and their stories were driven to be more dark and mature, which I began exploring off site and this prompted me to stay away from neopets for a few years (around 2012-2015), to not only explore my passion with more violent and real characters but also to hone my writing skilIs.
